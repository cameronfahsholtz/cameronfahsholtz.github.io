Blinking LEDs Project

This shows three red LEDs blinking from top to bottom one after another alternating every 1/10th of a second.

This picture shows the circuit.

This is the schematic for the circuit. The resistance was calculated in the following way. v = (Power source) - (Voltage Drop for LED) v = 5V -1.8V = 3.2V I = 30 mA (Max current through red LED) R = V/I = 3.2V/O.03A = 106.7 Ohms Because of these calculations, I know that I must use a resister that is larger than 106.7 Ohms. I decided to use a 220 Ohms resisters because of this.
