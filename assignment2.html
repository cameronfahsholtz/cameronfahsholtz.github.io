Fade LEDs and Button Project

This GIF shows a button that controls both a red and yellow LED and a tri-color LED that fades from red to green. When the button is being pressed, all LEDs turn on.

This picture shows arduino and the circuit.

This is the schematic for the circuit. The resistance for the tri-color LED was calculated in the following way. v = (Power source) - (Voltage Drop for LED) v = 5V -1.8V = 3.2V I = 30 mA (Max current through red LED) R = V/I = 3.2V/O.03A = 106.7 Ohms Because of these calculations, I know that I must use a resister that is larger than 106.7 Ohms. I decided to use a 220 Ohms resisters because of this. The resistance for the two LEDs in series was calculated using the same logic as above. I knew that a 220 Ohms resistor would also be larger than what two LEDs in series would need. I used a 220 Ohms resister for the pushbutton as well.
